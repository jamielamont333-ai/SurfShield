FAQ

Common questions

Does SurfShield record me through my camera?
No. Frames from your camera are analyzed and immediately discarded. Nothing is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere except briefly to our detection service to check whether anyone is watching. We never see your face.
What happens if my internet drops out?
SurfShield will pause detection and let you know. We're working on an on-device fallback so basic protection works offline too.
Will this drain my battery?
Camera use is light — we sample at long intervals and use efficient image compression. On laptops, expect minimal battery impact during normal work sessions.
Can SurfShield tell my face from a stranger's?
No, and that's deliberate. SurfShield never identifies who's in frame — it only counts people. If two or more people are visible, your screen blurs. This means we never collect or process any facial recognition data.
What happens after the 7-day free trial?
You'll be billed $1.99/month and continue uninterrupted. Cancel any time before day 7 and you'll never be charged. Cancel later and you'll keep access through the end of your billing period.
