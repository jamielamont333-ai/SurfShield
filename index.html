02 / How it sees

Built like a shield, not a screenshot.

F-01
Two-face detection

A vision model watches the framing of the room. The instant a second pair of eyes lines up with the screen, the pixels go.

F-02
Pixel dissolve

Not a black box — your screen pixelates progressively, intense enough to hide content but soft enough to keep you in flow.

F-03
Privacy-first by design

Frames are sent to a hardened API endpoint, processed for detection only, and never stored. Your camera feed is never recorded.

F-04
Adjustable sensitivity

Crank it up in cafes and on flights, relax it at your desk. Set the threshold that matches where you actually work.

F-05
Soft-dismiss

When the room clears, the screen comes back the moment you do. No interrupted flow, no extra clicks to recover.

F-06
Browser & Android

Works in any modern browser today, with Android app shipping soon. One subscription covers every device with a camera you use.
