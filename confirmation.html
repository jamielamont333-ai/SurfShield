Quick start

Get protected in 4 steps

  1. 1

    Install SurfShield

    Run the installer above. Windows may show a "SmartScreen" warning — click "More info" → "Run anyway." This is normal for indie apps without a $300/yr code-signing certificate.

  2. 2

    Open and grant camera access

    SurfShield needs your camera to see when someone's looking. We never record video, store images, or recognize faces.

  3. 3

    Click "Enable Shield"

    SurfShield runs quietly in your system tray. Right-click the tray icon anytime to manage settings or quit.

  4. 4

    Get back to work

    That's it. SurfShield watches in the background. If someone looks over your shoulder, your screen blurs until they look away.
